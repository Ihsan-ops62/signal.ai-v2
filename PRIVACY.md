# Privacy Policy for AI News Reporter

**Last updated:** [Date]

## Data Collection
AI News Reporter only stores your LinkedIn access token to post content on your behalf. We do not read, store, or share any of your personal data beyond what is required to publish posts you explicitly approve.

## Data Usage
Your LinkedIn token is encrypted and stored securely. It is used solely to:
- Publish posts you request
- Delete posts you request

## Data Deletion
You can disconnect your LinkedIn account at any time via the Settings page, which permanently deletes your stored token.
